Memberships

New members are always welcome at Terenure CC, simply contact the numbers listed in the Contact Us for further information. Training sessions will begin on the first Tuesday of April from 6pm, in the ground in Terenure Sports Club and will continue on Tuesdays and Thursdays right throughout the season until September. Prospective members of any standard are encouraged to come along to any training session and make themselves known to those in attendance, there is no requirement to have kit as a new member, we will loan gear that can be used to anyone who doesn't already have their own.
